TRIX Indicator Crew AI Investment decision Project

The TRIX Indicator Module enhances the AI Agent Stock Prediction system by applying a triple exponential moving average to raw price data, effectively filtering noise while preserving true momentum shifts. I developed and refined the TRIX calculation algorithm, integrated flexible parameter controls, and orchestrated CrewAI-driven analysis tasks to generate clear, actionable buy, sell, or hold signals.

The challenge addressed high-frequency volatility in market data: single or double smoothing often failed to suppress false signals, while simple momentum measures struggled with lag. The TRIX methodology—calculating rate-of-change on a triple-smoothed average—strikes a balance by reducing whipsaws and emphasizing sustained trend movements. My solution included dynamic EMA lengths, signal line crossovers, optional smoothing, and a decision pipeline that contextualizes TRIX values against current market price.
